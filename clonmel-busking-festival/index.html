Welcome to the Clonmel Busking Festival




The Clonmel Busking festival runs for four days every August. Its main aims are to provide high quality FREE music events for Clonmel town.

Busking takes place during the day in Clonmel town centre, while at night a number a concerts take place in various venues throughout the town.

The Busking festival is run by a small group of people dedicated to creating a music festival in Clonmel, which in years to come will rival Irelands biggest festivals, while also generating money and tourism for the local economy in Clonmel.

All the people involved in organizing the event, work on a voluntary basis. All funds raised through sponsorship are put into the running of the festival.
